Ryomen Sukuna is the primary antagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen, known as the "King of Curses." 
He was a human sorcerer from the Heian Era over 1,000 years ago who became a monstrously powerful, sadistic, and ancient cursed object after death. 
Sukuna inhabits the body of protagonist Yuji Itadori after he consumes one of his 20 indestructible, finger-shaped cursed objects.
